Abbe Pierre

The man who founded the Emmaus movement and helped the poor, the homeless, and the refugees.

Abbé Pierre becomes MP of Meurthe-et-Moselle: he wears the priestly cloth but doesn’t have the priestly speech. He will be re-elected twice (from 1945 to 1946 and from 1946 to 1951).
